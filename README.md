📚 StudyLend

On-chain student savings pools & microloans (SEA)

🧠 Overview

StudyLend is a decentralized savings and lending platform designed for student organizations in Southeast Asia.

It enables students to pool funds (paluwagan-style) and access instant microloans without relying on informal lenders or damaging personal relationships.

🚨 Problem

Students frequently face short-term financial gaps, especially before critical academic events.

Example:
A nursing student in Cebu needs ₱2,000 for review materials two weeks before board exams.

No stipend yet
Informal lenders charge 20% weekly interest
Borrowing from friends leads to no structure + broken trust
💡 Solution

StudyLend introduces on-chain student savings pools powered by Soroban smart contracts.

Student organizations create shared pools
Members deposit stablecoins (USDC)
Members can request instant microloans
Smart contract enforces:
Loan caps
Interest rates
Repayment rules

✅ No collateral
✅ Transparent
✅ No IOUs or social friction

⚙️ How It Works (MVP Flow)
1. Treasurer creates pool (max cap + interest)
2. Members deposit USDC into the contract
3. Student requests loan (request_loan)
4. USDC is disbursed instantly (<5s)
5. Student repays with interest
6. Pool grows for future borrowers
🔗 Key Features
🏦 On-chain Paluwagan – Shared savings pool
⚡ Instant Microloans – No approval delays
📜 Smart Contract Enforcement – No trust issues
💸 Low Interest – ~2% flat vs 5-6 lenders
🔍 Transparent Ledger – All transactions verifiable
